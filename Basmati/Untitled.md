# Basmati Rice with Scrambled Eggs and Tuna

**Chef:** Moazzam

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup Basmati rice
- 1.5 cups water (for cooking)
- Cold water (for soaking and rinsing)
- 2 fresh eggs
- 1 can of tuna (packed in oil or water)
- 1 tablespoon cooking oil
- Salt (to taste, optional)

---

## Preparation

### 1. Soak the Rice
1. Retrieve the sealed package of Basmati rice and open it.
2. Measure 1 cup of dry rice using a measuring cup.
3. Place the rice in a mixing bowl and fill with cold water until the water level is at least 2 cm above the rice.
4. Let the rice soak for exactly 60 minutes.
5. After soaking, drain the water using a fine-mesh strainer.
6. Return the drained rice to the bowl.

### 2. Rinse the Rice
1. Cover the rice with fresh cold water.
2. Gently agitate the rice for 10 seconds to rinse off excess starch.
3. Drain through a fine-mesh strainer.
4. Repeat the rinsing process a second time.
5. Ensure the draining water runs clear, then let the strainer sit for 2 minutes to drain excess water.

### 3. Cook the Rice
1. Transfer the cleaned rice to a cooking pot.
2. Add 1.5 cups of fresh water.
3. Place the pot on a stovetop burner over high heat and bring to a rolling boil.
4. Cover with a tight-fitting lid and immediately reduce the heat to the lowest setting.
5. Cook for exactly 12 minutes without lifting the lid.
6. Turn off the heat and let the pot steam for another 5 minutes with the lid on.
7. Remove the lid and fluff the rice with a fork.

### 4. Prepare the Eggs
1. Crack 2 eggs into a small bowl.
2. Whisk until the yolks and whites are fully blended into a uniform yellow liquid.

### 5. Prepare the Tuna
1. Open the can of tuna and drain all excess oil or water.
2. Flake the tuna into small pieces using a fork.

### 6. Combine and Serve
1. Heat 1 tablespoon of cooking oil in a frying pan over medium heat for 1 minute.
2. Pour the whisked eggs into the pan and scramble until soft curds form.
3. Add the cooked Basmati rice to the pan with the scrambled eggs.
4. Add the flaked tuna to the pan.
5. Gently fold and stir all ingredients together over medium-low heat for 2 minutes.
6. Turn off the heat and transfer the finished mixture to a serving plate.
